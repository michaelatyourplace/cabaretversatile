Best-Known Burlesque Shows Nationwide

Dita Von Teese

The undisputed queen of neo-burlesque, Dita Von Teese's opulent and stylized shows are a global phenomenon. She is renowned for her elaborate costumes, iconic martini glass routine, and a dedication to the glamour and theatrics of old Hollywood. Her performances are often large-scale, touring productions.

Venue: Major theaters and venues worldwide

Tour Dates
The Burlesque Hall of Fame Weekend

The premier event in the burlesque world, this annual festival in Las Vegas brings together the best performers from across the globe. It's a four-day celebration featuring showcases, competitions, educational panels, and the crowning of new burlesque royalty. It's a must-attend for any serious fan.

Venue: The Orleans Hotel & Casino, Las Vegas, NV

Learn More
The Lalas Burlesque Show

This all-female touring production, founded by celebrity choreographer Erin Lamont, delivers a high-energy, comedic, and glamorous experience. The show features top-tier dancers and a blend of classic burlesque with modern pop culture references, making it a favorite for bachelorette parties and a fun night out.

Venue: Touring nationwide
