Meet the Artists

Madame Rouge

The undisputed Queen of Charleston Burlesque, Madame Rouge is a force of nature on stage. Known for her impeccable fan dancing and stunning corsetry, her performances are a masterful blend of elegance and raw passion. With over a decade of experience, she's a true veteran of the stage, and her acts often tell complex, emotional stories.

Fleur de Lis

With a playful wink and a mischievous grin, Fleur de Lis brings a dose of whimsy to every stage she graces. Her specialty is comedic burlesque, where she uses props, timing, and a healthy dose of sass to get the audience laughing. Fleur is a breath of fresh air and a reminder that burlesque is about joy and humor as much as it is about sensuality.

Captain Cinder

Captain Cinder defies expectations with high-energy, theatrical acts that often incorporate fire-eating and other daring stunts. His performances are a blend of masculine power and delicate movements, challenging traditional notions of burlesque. Cinder is a true innovator, constantly pushing his creative and physical limits to deliver unforgettable performances.
